Privacy Policy

ClutchSpot · Last updated: 21 July 2026

ClutchSpot is a community app where players share links to their best gaming moments from YouTube, vote on clips, and compete in weekly leaderboards. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

The short version: we store your account, your username, the clips you post, and your votes. We never see or store your password or payment details. You can delete everything from inside the app at any time.

What we collect

DataWhy
Account identifier, display name, profile photo, email address To create your account and show who posted a clip. Provided by Google, Apple, or by you when signing up with email.
Username you choose Shown publicly on your clips and on the leaderboard.
Clips you post (YouTube link, title, game) To display them in the feed and the weekly competition. Public.
Your votes To count scores and enforce one vote per person. Not shown publicly as individual votes.
Purchase records (boosts) To apply the boost you paid for and to remove ads from your account.

We do not collect: your password (authentication is handled by Apple, Google, or Firebase), your payment card details, your contacts, your photos, your precise location, or your health data.

Who processes your data

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database hosting. Your account and clips are stored on Firebase servers.
  • Apple / Google Sign-In — only if you choose those sign-in methods. They tell us your name, email, and profile photo. If you use "Hide My Email" with Apple, we only ever receive the relay address.
  • RevenueCat — processes in-app purchases. It receives your account identifier and the purchase receipt from Apple.
  • Yandex Advertising Network — shows ads in the app. It may access your device's advertising identifier. Ads are removed permanently once you buy a boost.
  • YouTube (Google) — clips play through YouTube's embedded player. YouTube may set its own cookies and collect viewing data under Google's privacy policy.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with anyone beyond the services listed above.

What is public

Your username, profile photo, the clips you post, and their scores are visible to everyone using the app. Your email address is never shown to other users. Individual votes are private — only the totals are public.

Deleting your data

Open the app, go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, your username reservation, and every clip you posted. It cannot be undone. If you'd rather we do it, email us and we'll handle it within 30 days.

Children

ClutchSpot is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

Security

Data is stored on Google Firebase with server-side security rules that restrict who can read and write what. Sign-in is handled by Apple, Google, or Firebase Authentication — we never handle your password directly.

Changes

If this policy changes in a way that affects you, we'll update the date at the top of this page and, where it matters, tell you in the app.
