Community Guidelines

MemoSip · Effective date: July 19, 2026

MemoSip works best when drink experiences are honest, useful, and respectful. These rules apply to profiles, photos, ratings, notes, lists, and every other community surface.

Share genuine experiences

  • Rate the drink itself and describe what you actually tried.
  • Post photos you took or have permission to use.
  • Do not manipulate ratings, coordinate fake engagement, or impersonate another person or business.

Keep people safe

  • No threats, harassment, hate, bullying, sexual exploitation, self-harm encouragement, or graphic violence.
  • No private personal information, including addresses, phone numbers, financial details, or private conversations.
  • No promotion of dangerous drinking, underage alcohol use, illegal drugs, or sales of regulated goods.

Respect rights and the community

  • No copyright or trademark infringement, spam, scams, malware, or misleading commercial promotion.
  • No obscene or sexually explicit content.
  • Follow local laws and venue rules.

Report and block

Use Report Entry for content that may violate these rules and Block User to hide an account from your feed. For urgent assistance, email mstfkuk@gmail.com with the entry or profile details.

Enforcement

We may remove content, reduce visibility, restrict features, suspend accounts, or permanently terminate accounts depending on severity, repetition, context, and legal obligations.
