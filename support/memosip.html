MemoSip Support

Official help center · Last updated: July 19, 2026

MemoSip is a visual drink journal and discovery community. Rate the drink—not just the place—save tasting notes, remember where you had it, and discover what to try next.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

You can continue as a guest. You may also use Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In to keep your account available across supported devices.

How do I add a drink?

Tap the add button, take a photo or choose one from your library, then enter the drink name, rating, tasting notes, amount, price, venue, and visibility. Only the drink name is required.

Where are photos stored?

Before upload, MemoSip resizes your selected image to a compact JPEG. The encoded image is stored with your drink entry in Google Firebase Firestore. MemoSip does not use Firebase Storage.

Who can see my entries?

Choose Public, Friends, or Only Me when creating an entry. Public entries may appear in community and discovery surfaces. Only Me entries are available only to your signed-in account.

Why does MemoSip request camera or photo access?

Camera access is used only when you take a drink photo or scan a barcode. Photo-library access is used only when you choose an existing image. You can still create an entry without a photo.

Why does MemoSip request location access?

Location access is optional and is used only when you choose a nearby venue or map feature. MemoSip does not use background location tracking.

Are caffeine, sugar, calorie, or alcohol values medical advice?

No. Any nutrition or ingredient figures are estimates that can vary by recipe, serving size, brand, and venue. MemoSip does not provide medical advice.

How do I report content or block a user?

Open an entry and choose Report Entry or Block User. Reported content may be reviewed and removed. Blocking immediately hides that user's content from your feed.

Delete your account and data

In MemoSip, open Profile → Settings → Delete My Account and All Data, then confirm. This permanently deletes your Firebase Authentication account, profile, drink entries, lists, likes, follows, and blocks associated with your account. This action cannot be undone.

If you cannot access the app, email mstfkuk@gmail.com from the address associated with your account. We may ask for limited information to verify ownership before processing the request.

Privacy choices

  • Choose the visibility of every drink entry.
  • Deny camera, photo-library, or location permission in device settings.
  • Report content and block users from an entry page.
  • Delete your account and associated data from inside the app.
  • Request privacy assistance by email.
