Common questions

How do the weekly competitions work?

Every clip belongs to the week it was posted in (Monday to Sunday). Each game — Valorant, League of Legends, Dota 2, Counter-Strike 2 — has its own leaderboard. At the end of the week, the top clip in each game becomes Clip of the Week and gets pinned to the winner's profile permanently. Ties go to whoever reached the score first.

Why did my score change by two?

If you had already voted up and then vote down, your upvote is removed and a downvote is added — so the total moves by two. That's normal.

My video says "unavailable" in the app

Some YouTube uploaders disable playback outside YouTube. When that happens, we show an "Open on YouTube" button instead. There's nothing wrong with your clip — but if you want it to play inside ClutchSpot, upload a version with embedding allowed.

What does a boost do?

A boost puts your clip at the top of the feed, labelled "Boosted", for the duration you bought. It does not change your score or your place in the weekly leaderboard — those stay fully decided by real votes. Buying any boost also removes ads from your account permanently.

I bought a boost but don't see it

Open the boost screen and tap Restore Purchases. If it still doesn't appear, email us with the date of purchase and we'll sort it out.

Can I change my username?

Yes — Profile → Change username. Your old clips update to the new name automatically. Usernames are unique, so the one you want may already be taken.

How do I delete my account?

Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, your username, and every clip you posted. It can't be undone.

How do I get a refund?

Purchases go through Apple, so refunds do too. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com. If a boost you paid for didn't work, email us first and we'll make it right.
