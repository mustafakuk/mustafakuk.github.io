Boosts and payments

A boost buys visibility, not ranking. Your clip appears at the top of the feed with a "Boosted" label for the duration you purchased. It does not change your score or your position in the weekly leaderboard. Every vote still comes from a real person.
  • Boosts are sold as one-time purchases through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Prices are shown in the app before you buy.
  • Buying any boost also removes ads from your account permanently.
  • If several clips are boosted at the same time, the top slot rotates between them so everyone gets a fair share of the exposure.
  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.
